Chip in to Save the World

The Challenge

The methane emissions from the Arctic may already be enough to doom humanity even if we drop our carbon emissions to zero now.

The Big Project

Grow algae, turn it into charcoal, recycle the nutrients, remediate the water outflow, pile up the carbon.

The Immediate Project

The big unknown is recycling phosphorus, which is a limiting factor in the biosphere. The immediate project is a couple of undergrads (under supervision) growing algae, turning it into charcoal, and doing chemical analyses on the results, with a particular focus on phosphorus and its compounds.
